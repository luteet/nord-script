Our clients seek business process automation, not just a programming process

The objective isn't the app, it's the solution the app provides.

That's why our projects are overseen by business analysts from the research department, rather than it project managers.

Your Goal

If your goal is a developed piece of software that matches your predetermined specifications,

then choose any IT company. In this case, you are responsible for the final result, while the company you work with is responsible only for the programming process.

But if your goal is to automate a specific business process,

then choose a specialized IT company, because organizing and streamlining a specific process requires a fundamentally different approach than that of broad-spectrum contractors.

We look forward to assisting you with our unique approach to business automation

We craft software to manage various business processes

From procurement management to waste processing, telecom tower maintenance, manufacturing time tracking, production planning and barcode-driven inventory control – our expertise covers a broad spectrum of needs.

How we determine the cost to our client

  • 1. FREE PRELIMINARY RESEARCH

    Following our preliminary research and concept development, we are happy to provide you with a price range. These initial steps are conducted at our expense.

    Once the preliminary research is complete, you'll receive a price range along with our vision for your project's general concept

  • 2. IN-DEPTH RESEARCH

    If you're satisfied with the concept, price range and project timeline, we'll proceed with in-depth research and preparation of detailed technical specifications. You'll receive not only a comprehensive project brief but also an accurate project budget. The fee for this service is 10% of the total project cost.

    As a result, you'll receive the project's final cost along with detailed technical specs
